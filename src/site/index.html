Global Accessibility Awareness Day (GAAD) is a celebration of digital accessibility and coming together. GAAD is a global movement to get people thinking, talking and learning about digital accessibility. Its about ensuring that everyone, regardless of ability can access and experience digital content and experiences.

Join us at Solirius Reply for our GAAD event, where we have put together a series of sessions covering various aspects of accessibility within digital spaces. Whether you are looking to upskill your delivery teams, understand how accessibility fits into agile transformation, tackle specific challenges, or just learn more about accessibility, our specialists will be on hand to help!
