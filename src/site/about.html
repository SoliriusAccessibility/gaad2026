At Solirius Reply, accessibility is our daily practice. Through years of helping organisations improve their digital accessibility services, we have built up a deep experitse across all areas of the digital accessibility process. This year, for GAAD, we have drawn from our expertise to offer talks on a range of subjects, to help you feel more confident in delivering accessible experiences.

Meet the speakers

Our speakers offer fun, insightful and wide ranging approaches to accessibility. Our team has a variety of skills across all things digital. Get to know a bit more about our speakers, who will present their knowledge and experience at our GAAD event.

Rowan Godfrey

Test Engineer and Accessibility Consultant

With a background in international education, Rowan now works as a QA tester, championing accessibility and helping teams build empathy to create inclusive digital products.

Ben Gaunt

Accessibility Analyst

Ben is an Accessibility Analyst who combines a background in creative computing and UX. He enjoys learning and sharing knowledge about hands-on testing processes to help build more inclusive digital spaces.

Aaron Grey

Accessibility and UX Specialist

Aaron is an accessibility consultant with a UX and creative background. He specialises in manual testing with assistive technologies and partnering with User-Centered Design (UCD) to support teams in embedding inclusive practices from the outset.

Gillian Fatica

Accessibility Specialist

Accessibility specialist and inclusive design advocate with a background in high-tech UX and customer-facing digital services. Empowering teams through expert auditing, training, and strategic advice to ensure digital services are designed for everyone.

Hannah Cox

Accessibility Specialist

Hannah enjoys building accessible systems and components alongside front-end and design teams. Using her experience in business software, she provides hands-on support to bridge the gap between technical standards and real user impact.
